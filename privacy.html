Summary

GSCam does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. The app works primarily on-device. Recorded videos are saved to your device’s Photos library when you choose to save them.

Permissions Used

  • Camera: to display and record the camera feed.
  • Microphone (optional): to record audio with video.
  • Photos: to save recorded videos to your Photos library.

You can change permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

Data Collection and Sharing

  • Collected data: None.
  • Server communication: None required for core features.
  • Third-party analytics/ads: Not used.

The app may store settings locally on your device (e.g., last selected options). You can remove app data by uninstalling the app.

Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
