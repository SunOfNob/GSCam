Quick Start

  1. Launch the app. When asked for Camera access, tap “Allow”.
  2. Tap Load PLY and select the model you want to display (loaded from the in‑app PlyModels folder).
  3. Adjust the placement using the Move / Rotate / Scale buttons.
  4. Choose recording settings (e.g., 24fps / 30fps, 1920×1080 / 3840×1080).
  5. Tap REC to start recording. Tap again to stop.
  6. When recording finishes, the video is saved to Photos (Photo Library).

Note: To record audio, you must also allow Microphone access.

FAQ

Q. Is any data sent to a server?

A. No. The app is designed to run entirely on-device. Recordings are saved to the Photos app (see the Privacy Policy for details).

Q. AR tracking is unstable

A. Use a well-lit area and slowly move the device while pointing at textured surfaces like floors or walls. Tracking may be unstable in the dark, with plain white walls only, or during fast movement.

Q. Recording is slow / stutters

A. Try lowering the resolution (1920×1080) and/or fps (24). If the model is heavy, switching to a lighter PLY can help.

Q. The video is not saved to Photos

A. Allow Photos access in iOS Settings:
Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos, then check GSCam’s permission.

Troubleshooting

  • Camera is black: Allow Camera permission → enable Camera for GSCam in iOS Settings.
  • No audio: Allow Microphone permission. Also make sure the device microphone isn’t blocked.
  • Model does not appear: Confirm the PLY is in PlyModels and is a supported format.
  • Recording stops unexpectedly: Free up storage space. High-resolution recording uses a lot of space.
