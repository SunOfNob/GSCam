How to Use

1. Start Recording

Tap the red "REC" button on the main screen to start recording. Recording time will be displayed at the top of the screen.

2. Change Settings

Tap the settings button in the top right corner to access the settings screen. Adjust resolution, frame rate, and other parameters.

3. Save Data

Recorded videos are automatically saved to your Photo Library. You can also manage data within the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to grant camera access permission?

A: Yes, GSCam requires access to your camera and photo library to capture photos and videos. Please grant permission when prompted on first launch.

Q: Where are the recorded videos saved?

A: Recorded videos are saved to your device's Photo Library. You can view them in the Photos app.

Q: How much storage space is required?

A: Storage requirements vary depending on recording duration and resolution. We recommend ensuring sufficient free space for extended recordings.

Q: What should I do if the app won't launch?

A: Try restarting your device. If the problem persists, try reinstalling the app.

Required Permissions

  • Camera: Required to capture photos and videos
  • Photo Library: Required to save and access captured data

System Requirements

  • iOS 13.0 or later
  • iPhone compatible
  • Camera-equipped device
